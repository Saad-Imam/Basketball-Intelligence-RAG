### Basketball RAG Test Queries

#### Simple Retrieval (Fact-Based)
1. What are the exact dimensions of an NBA free throw lane?
2. How many personal fouls lead to a player's disqualification in an NBA game?
3. What is the official definition of a '1 on 1' action according to hoopstudent?
4. What is the required air pressure (in pounds) for an official NBA basketball?
5. Define the 'skunk rule' as it applies to informal pickup basketball.
6. What are the specific dimensions and transparency requirements for an NBA backboard?
7. What does the 'winner’s take' rule (or 'make it, take it') mean in a 1v1 game?
8. What is the primary purpose of the 'zipper cut' in an offensive set?
9. List the four game officials required for an NBA game according to the rulebook.
10. What is the 'Restricted Area' on an NBA court and how far is it from the basket?

#### Semantic Retrieval (Conceptual & Strategy)

12. Explain the difference between 'speed' and 'quickness' from a defensive perspective.
13. Why is the 'high post' often called the 'nail' area in defensive formations?
14. What is the underlying logic for requiring a 'clear' behind the three-point line in half-court games?
15. How does the 1-2-2 press function as a 'containment' defense compared to more aggressive traps?
16. Describe the concept of 'attacking small and retreating big' when faking a trap.
17. What are the main vulnerabilities of a 1-3-1 zone defense when facing a skilled passing team?
18. Explain the 'bumping' technique used by zone defenders to counter ball reversal.
19. How does an 'overload' strategy help an offense break down a standard zone defense?
20. What is the role of a 'safety' player when an offensive team is trying to break a full-court press?

#### Logical & Scenario-Based (Fan, Player, and Coach Perspectives)

24. "Strategic Rotation: In a 1-1-3 zone, if the ball is skipped from the right corner to the left wing, which defender is responsible for the initial closeout?"
25. "Casual Fan Question: We're playing '21' at the park. If I make a 3-pointer, how many points do I get and how many free throws follow?"
26. "Defensive Logic: Why would a coach instruct a defender to 'three-quarter front' a low post player instead of playing directly behind them?"
27. "Scenario: A player gets a defensive rebound in a pickup game but forgets to clear the ball before scoring. What is the resulting call?"
28. "Court Awareness: If a coach crosses the midcourt line during live play to argue a call, what is the specific penalty assessed?"
29. "Offensive Application: How does using a zipper cut *before* an on-ball screen make the screen harder to defend than just running the screen alone?"
30. "Game Management: What happens if a player's jersey is pulled and it results in an 'altercation'—who makes the final ruling, the on-court officials or the Replay Center?"
